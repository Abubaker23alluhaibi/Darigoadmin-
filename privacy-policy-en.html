Introduction

Welcome to the "Dari go" Real Estate App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us when using the app.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • User Type (Individual or Real Estate Agency)
  • License Number (for Real Estate Agencies)
  • Profile Picture (optional)

2. Property Information

  • Property details added by you
  • Images uploaded for properties
  • Geographic location of properties
  • Contact information associated with properties

3. Usage Information

  • Activity logs in the app
  • Favorite properties
  • Search and filter operations
  • Messages sent and received

4. Technical Information

  • IP Address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique device identifier
  • Network information

How We Use Information

1. Providing Services

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Displaying available properties
  • Facilitating communication between users
  • Managing favorites and notifications

2. App Improvement

  • Analyzing usage to improve services
  • Fixing bugs and improving performance
  • Developing new features

3. Communication

  • Sending important notifications
  • Responding to inquiries
  • Sending app updates

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following cases:

1. With Your Explicit Consent

  • When communicating with property owners
  • When sharing contact information

2. With Service Providers

  • Hosting and cloud providers
  • Analytics services (anonymized)
  • Payment services (if implemented)

3. For Legal Purposes

  • Compliance with local laws
  • Protecting our legal rights
  • Preventing fraud or illegal activities

Information Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your information:

1. Encryption

  • Data encryption during transmission and storage
  • HTTPS usage for all communications
  • Strong password protection

2. Access Control

  • Limited access to sensitive information
  • Regular review of permissions
  • Staff training on data protection

3. Monitoring and Protection

  • Continuous monitoring of suspicious activities
  • Regular backups of data
  • Incident response plan

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

1. Access and Review

  • Request a copy of your personal information

  • Verify accuracy of information

2. Modification and Deletion

  • Update your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and information

3. Privacy Control

  • Modify privacy settings
  • Unsubscribe from notifications
  • Request deletion of specific information

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use various technologies to improve your experience:

1. Cookies

  • Essential cookies to ensure app functionality
  • Analytics cookies to understand usage
  • Preference cookies to save your settings

2. Tracking Technologies

  • Unique identifiers for devices
  • Session and activity tracking
  • Performance and usage analysis

Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

1. Personal Information

  • Throughout the duration of your active account

2. Property Information

3. System Logs

  • One year for security logs
